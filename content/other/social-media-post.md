- [Wild Violet Fibers][wvf], is promoting collaborative short story projects. 

- To keep the process more organized going forward, the business has adopted some changes to help with tracking progress between multiple writers, the business owner, and the new editor-in-chief.

# Colorwheel Project - Call for Writers Form
## Hello writers!

We have a new batch of short story slots open for the Colorwheel project. Please feel free to check out some of the new short stories that have been published there!

### What’s new about this process

- We’re using Jira to help track story progress and send out automatic reminder emails. This form will collect your email address and a link to the document with your stories.

- You should receive notification and reminder emails for upcoming deadlines, and which colorways you are writing for.

- Caitlin is officially onboard as our resident Editor-in-Chief! She will provide feedback on everyone's stories to help you create wonderful sensory experiences for our readers.

- Jin is helping out with the fun case tracking stuff. (He recently wrote for _[Afternoon Tea][afternoon-tea]_ and _[London Fog][london-fog]_.)

### The colorwheel story slots up for grabs

_Initial drafts are due 1 week before the final draft deadline._

| Color pair | Drafts due |
|------------|------------|
| Pair #1    | Date 1     |
| Pair #2    | Date 2     |
| Pair #3    | Date 3     |

If you have any questions about or feedback on the new process, please let us know! We're currently less than one-third through the whole project, and would love to continue improving the collaboration experience for all writers.

_End of draft_
